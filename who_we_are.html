Meet Nick, our founder

Nick Sinclair is intimately familiar with the importance of accountants and the value that they provide.

In December of 20--, ---- founded AGILE AC to expand his own accounting and financial planning business. He established a remote accounting team in the Philippines and almost immediately began to see the benefits. By outsourcing some of his accounting.

In April of 2014, Nick – armed with knowledge of the pitfalls and mistakes accounting firm owners may encounter as they consider a global outsourcing strategy, and knowing how to avoid them – spoke at a roadshow about the benefits of outsourcing in the Philippines.

From there, other accounting firms wanted to place their staff with Nick’s team, and AGILE started offering external outsourced accounting services to clients in the industry. Within a few months, AGILE’s overall team grew to 103 team members serving 29 accounting firms in Australia.
